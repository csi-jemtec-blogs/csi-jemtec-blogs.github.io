ERA OF 5G TECHNOLOGY

Arushi Verma
04 August, 2021 | 1 minute, 17 seconds

The methods of communication between humans, their methods of acquiring knowledge, working, thinking and other interactions have all changed in recent years due to the availability of the internet supported by the worldwide mobile revolution. But the current work ethos and expectations of people demand a better technology in which they can connect to multiple wireless technologies,networks,terminals and applications,all simultaneously.So this latest technology has been named as 5G,or 5th generation wireless systems.

5G networks are advanced mobile communication technology networks which will have low latency.These networks are designed to support operations that require almost real-time access to rapidly changing data. Some of the characteristics of and performance expected from such networks includes: Upto 10gbps, very low power consumption by devices and very high network reliability though the use of superior router and switch technology.

But with this, there are number of challenges in deploying the 5g networks. New equipment with faster computing and data transfer ability will need to be developed by the mobile service providers.In addition, new software applications will need to be developed to provide the additional functionality which 5G provides. Thus, even when 5G networks are deployed, it will take the telecom companies about two years to achieve the speeds and functionality 5G promises.

A bright future awaits India after implementation of 5g networks, as this technology will enhance the capabilities of the people significantly by taking care of routine tasks. The analytical Indian minds can then start finding solutions to more complex problems such as rising environmental pollution,effective management of waste and other similar issues.
